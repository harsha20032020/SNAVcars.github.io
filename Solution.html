Solution

What are the details?


We aim to solve this problem by creating an application/website to connect potential customers for renting cars and the owners/leasers.

 We will also collect a necessary deposit amount from the customer and the required documents to facilitate the same. Our highest priority will be the safety of both the car and the individual.

The owner must have his/her car insured, and the renter must submit all of his personal details-Driving license, bank account details, as well as a small security deposit.

We are exploring options where we can have both short-term and long-term renting solutions available.

Initially, we would start with cars but we’re also looking to tap into the two-wheeler market after dominating the car rental segment.

       

How is it effective?


We think this is an effective solution since there is an ever-increasing demand for transportation. 

We are looking to fill the demand, where most people buy cars out of social pressure and don't use them as much, and those who want to use a car regularly but are unable to afford their own.

This also tackles other issues like reducing the carbon footprint (since the usage of existing cars is increased and the dependency on new vehicles is diminished) and generating revenue for the people whose cars collect dust and provide a safe and personal mode of transport for the working class.
